# Requirements

## Functional Requirements

### User Management
- Users can log in and log out
- Admin and cashier roles exist
- Users can change passwords

### Product Management
- Add, edit, delete products
- Assign products to categories
- Track quantity and pricing
- Search and filter products

### Category Management
- Add, edit, delete categories

### Sales Management
- Create transactions
- Add multiple items
- Compute totals and change
- Record daily sales

### Inventory Monitoring
- Real-time stock updates
- Low stock alerts
- Stock adjustments

### Utang Management
- Record customer credit
- Track balances
- Record payments

### Supplier & Purchases
- Add suppliers
- Record restocking
- Update stock automatically

### Expense Tracking
- Record expenses
- Calculate totals

### Reports
- Generate sales, inventory, and profit reports

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Secure authentication
- Fast system response
- Data backup support
- Mobile-friendly UI
